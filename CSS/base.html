HISTORYA

A lover music making music

HISTORYA , a talented artist hailing from Mexico City, creates a captivating blend of urban, alternative, and regueton music. Emerging from a humble background in a poverty-stricken neighborhood, HISTORYA weaves his music with raw authenticity. His lyrics serve as a powerful reflection of his personal journey, delving into the depths of his childhood memories, life experiences as a gay individual, and the challenges he faced as a skilled construction worker. With each verse, HISTORYA's songs resonate with honesty, painting vivid pictures of resilience, identity, and the pursuit of dreams. Through his artistry, HISTORYA invites listeners to connect with his narrative and embrace their own stories of triumph and self-discovery.
